AICTE Approved and Affiliated To RGPV

Dr. Bhagat Singh Rai College, approved by AICTE and affiliated with RGPV, is dedicated to upholding the highest standards of technical education. In its 49th meeting held on 14th March 2017, the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) approved a comprehensive package of measures aimed at enhancing the standards of technical education across the country. These measures, mandated for all AICTE-approved technical institutions, are designed to ensure the provision of competent technical manpower essential for national development.
