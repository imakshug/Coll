DBSR College - at a Glance

  • Dr. Bhagat Singh Rai College: Leading the Way in Excellence

    Dr. Bhagat Singh Rai College, established in 2021, is a premier institution of pharmaceutical education located in Seoni. Named after the visionary Dr. Bhagat Singh Rai, the college is dedicated to providing top-notch education and equipping students with the skills and knowledge required to become highly proficient professionals in the field.

    Educational Excellence

    The college offers a wide range of courses tailored to meet the evolving needs of the pharmaceutical industry. With cutting-edge infrastructure and a highly qualified faculty, Dr. Bhagat Singh Rai College is committed to delivering a comprehensive and stimulating learning experience to its students. Our state-of-the-art laboratories, modern classrooms, and extensive library resources provide an optimal environment for learning and research. The curriculum is designed to ensure that students not only gain theoretical knowledge but also develop practical skills through hands-on training and industry exposure.

    Vision and Mission

    The vision of Dr. Bhagat Singh Rai College is to develop capable and socially responsible individuals who will play a significant role in advancing the industry. We aim to foster a culture of innovation, critical thinking, and ethical practices among our students. Our mission is to produce highly competent professionals who are ready to meet the challenges of the pharmaceutical sector and contribute positively to society.

    By choosing Dr. Bhagat Singh Rai College, students embark on a journey of academic excellence and professional growth, guided by a legacy of dedication and a commitment to quality education.

AICTE Approved and Affiliated To RGPV

Dr. Bhagat Singh Rai College, approved by AICTE and affiliated with RGPV, is dedicated to upholding the highest standards of technical education. In its 49th meeting held on 14th March 2017, the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) approved a comprehensive package of measures aimed at enhancing the standards of technical education across the country. These measures, mandated for all AICTE-approved technical institutions, are designed to ensure the provision of competent technical manpower essential for national development.

Commitment To Excellence

We believe our first responsibility is to the students and their parents, ensuring their needs are met with the highest quality education. Everything we do is aimed at maintaining and improving our standards. This includes purchasing new equipment, providing state-of-the-art facilities, conducting cutting-edge research, developing innovative ideas, and collaborating with industries.We strive to be knowledge creators rather than merely knowledge consumers. This means constantly training our teachers and staff, recognizing their merit, and respecting their dignity. They should have a sense of loyalty and receive fair and adequate compensation. We ensure a clean, orderly, and enjoyable environment, promoting an atmosphere of respect and dedication.
